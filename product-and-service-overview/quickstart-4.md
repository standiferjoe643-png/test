# Quick Comparison

| <p><br></p>                 | **Standard Migration**                                               | **Managed Migration**                             | **Custom Migration**                                                                          |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Who runs the migration?** | You                                                                  | Next-Cart                                         | Next-Cart                                                                                     |
| **Custom data & plugins**   | Not Included                                                         | Not Included                                      | Fully included                                                                                |
| **Effort on your side**     | Highest (you configure & run)                                        | Medium (you review & approve, Next-Cart operates) | Lowest (Next-Cart handles end-to-end, including custom parts)                                 |
| **Recommended scenarios**   | Native data, technically confident teams, budget-sensitive projects. | Native data, limited internal time/resources.     | Heavy plugin usage, complex business rules, and mission-critical data that must be preserved. |
